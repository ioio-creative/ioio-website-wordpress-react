Concord Music Hall
Rebranding Chicago's Most Eclectic Music Venue

A digitally-led rebrand centered around eclecticism

Branding, Web Design, UX, Development

At Concord Music Hall an emphasis is put on the quality & diversity of experiences patrons have at the venue. All are welcome & united by the love of live music

The word, “Concord” means a harmonious business relationship. 3 distinctly different, long-time Chicago promoters came together to build a unique venue in Chicago’s Logan Square. The result is an eclectic offering of performances that vary dramatically night-to-night. We aimed to create a brand platform built around that very idea. Much like the nightly experience at the venue, the brand expression is flexible, playful and morphs based on the application.
