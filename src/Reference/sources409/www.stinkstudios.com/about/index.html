  • Part of the Stink family

    We're part of Stink, one of the world's most diverse and awarded creative networks. Comprised of Stink Films and Stink Studios, Stink creates film, interactive and original content for clients across advertising, entertainment, music and the arts.

  • A global company with a local feel

    We launched in London in 2009 with a team of five. Today, we’re one of the world’s most awarded creative studios. We have offices in London, New York, Los Angeles, Paris, Shanghai, São Paulo, and Berlin, with a few more on the way.

  • A modern approach to content

    With our non-rostered approach to content creation, we take a different route to quality and craft. Our talented in-house team and our ever-expanding facilities allows us to be solutions-oriented whilst still retaining focus on the end product.

  • Work that invites active attention

    The internet is an attention economy, but not all attention is created equal. That’s why we invent, design and build digital campaigns and experiences that people truly want to spend time with. We call this “active attention” and we attract it with work that’s beautiful, entertaining, useful, and anything but boring.

  • Executionally-focused and solutions-oriented

    For us, craft isn’t just a set of skills; it’s a philosophy that informs everything we do. Making things isn’t the end of our process, it is our process.

  • A culture that focuses on the details

    The tiniest things can make the biggest difference. Whether it’s presentations, prototypes, insights, designs or code, we focus on the small details because that’s often where the magic is.

Products

From products with commercial applications to bespoke tools that we utilize for our clients, our technology team has created a suite of original IP over the years. Below is a sample of some of our most used products. If you're interested in knowing more, email us at products@stinkstudios.com.

  • RITAA platform to create personalized films at speed, scale and incredible quality

  • Responsive TVA new way to discover and enjoy content

  • SwitchboardA modern platform for DOOH deployment

Our RITA (Render In The Air) platform allows us to create unique shareable ultra HD videos for every user, delivered instantly to any device.

Our patented Responsive.TV platform allows users to adjust the length of a video to suit their context, all while maintaining narrative integrity. It does this magically, without buffering, repetition or interruption. By rendering content in a continuous stream rather than as individual videos, our front-end player also improves the content consumption experience. Joshua Topolsky called it "Tinder for video." For more information, email sales@responsive.tv

Switchboard is our campaign management tool that effortlessly facilitates omni-channel creative distribution and dynamic content delivery. The platform can be configured depending on the requirements of your campaign, ranging from simply playing postman with fresh copy, through to managing a multi-API integrated dynamic template or rendered asset campaign. Unlike other platforms, Switchboard operates on an HTML standard, meaning it’s the only future facing system of its kind.
