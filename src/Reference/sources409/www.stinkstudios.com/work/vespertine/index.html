Vespertine

Jordan Kahn
Websites Content Branding

A gastronomical experiment seeking to disrupt the course of the modern restaurant.

See the project

Vespertine was formed through a collaboration with architect Eric Owen Moss, and a group of renowned artists to re-imagine and explore the experience of dining. The convergence of food, art, architecture, music, and sculpture is woven throughout to create an immersive, multi-sensory event.

Stink Studios helped Chef Jordan Kahn announce and express his vision in the form of a visual identity, with a website and a film that serves as the site’s centerpiece.

Our logo design evokes the futurism of Chef Jordan Kahn’s cuisine, imagining its own binary language. We also designed printed material that included business cards, menus, check sleeves and a hand-bound wine list.
