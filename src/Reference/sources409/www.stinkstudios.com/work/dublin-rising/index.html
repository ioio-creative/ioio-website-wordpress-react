Dublin Rising

Google Ireland

Step back in time to the streets, events, and people who shaped history.

See the project

To commemorate the centenary of the Easter Rising in Dublin, we partnered with Google Ireland to retell the story of events that shaped a country, with narration from Colin Farrell.

Explore the history through an immersive video and audio experience across desktop, mobile and tablet. Using only Google Street View and archived material, we retold this important story in a way only Google can.
