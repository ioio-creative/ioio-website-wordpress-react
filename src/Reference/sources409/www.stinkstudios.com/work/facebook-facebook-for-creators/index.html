Announcing a global community for video creators

Facebook for Creators is a platform designed to help video creators learn, grow and find success on Facebook. Stink Studios was asked to create a film piece to introduce and promote the community at launch.

Directed by Stink Films' Samy Mosher, the film showcases the creativity of an eclectic mix of real creators, celebrating the human connections they build on Facebook.

Stink Studios also shot three additional “spotlight” creator interviews and crafted video ad units that drove traffic to the website.

We added the talented Mark Wickens to the shoot team to provide stills for a photo library.
