We're voting Remain

An important day for the UK creative community
James Britton June 23, 2016

Our team in London is made of talented people from no fewer than 9 European countries, with an average age that falls in the mid-twenties. We have producers from Croatia and France, visual effects designers from Sweden and Belgium, developers from Italy and Hungary, and designers from Portugal and Spain. The free flow of talent in (and out) of the UK has immeasurably improved the work we do for our UK and International clients alike.

If we vote to leave the EU, tens of thousands of talented people may leave the UK, and a future generation of talent may never arrive in the first place.

We’re voting #Remain today, and we hope the rest of the UK creative community will too.

— James Britton
