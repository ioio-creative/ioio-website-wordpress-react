Senior Producer

London

You will be a senior member of Stink Studios London, providing input into the strategic, creative, production and business development sides of our business.

About you

You represent our team to the client. You will be responsible for devising and negotiating budgets, and you will assemble and manage teams of developers, designers, creatives and producers in our studio to ensure that the atmosphere and the work are both excellent.

  • Be motivated to win new business and be entrepreneurial;
  • Plan, prioritize and successfully manage your workload across multiple projects and in turn, manage and support producers on your project;
  • Be current with industry trends, including specific creative projects, clients, and individual producers, creatives, designers and developers;
  • Be proactive, enthusiastic and have a passion for learning about and working on emergent technologies;
  • Enjoy working in a team environment and contribute to the team's ideas;

This position requires at least 5+ years of experience in producing large scale interactive experiences and campaigns, preferably in a production company or agency environment. Experience in film production is also a plus.
